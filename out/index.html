/ 05 — FAQ

Asked
often.

What's the minimum order quantity?

+
There isn't one. We do single trophies and 500-piece corporate gifting runs with the same care. Pricing scales with volume.

How long does a typical job take?

+
Small jobs — laser cuts, single trophies, photo frames — usually 2–4 working days. Signage installations and large UV prints are scoped after a brief.

Do you ship outside Bengaluru?

+
Yes. We regularly courier across India for trophies, awards and gifting. Signage installations are Bengaluru and surrounding areas.

Can I bring my own design?

+
Absolutely. Send a vector file (AI, PDF, SVG, DXF) or a clean reference image. We'll flag anything that needs adjusting before production.

Do you provide installation for signage?

+
Yes — site survey, fabrication, electricals and mounting are handled in-house for shopfront and façade signage in Bengaluru.

What materials do you work with?

+
Cast and extruded acrylic, MDF, ply, ACP, aluminium composite, stainless steel, PVC, and most rigid substrates that take a UV print.
